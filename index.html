Biography

Graph and Trustworthy Machine Learning Research Laboratory

Chuan Chen

(https://cse.sysu.edu.cn/content/2682)

E-mail: chenchuan@mail.sysu.edu.cn

Graph and Trustworthy Machine Learning Research Laboratory of Sun Yat-sen University (hereinafter referred to as the GTML-Lab) is committed to machine learning in following 3 areas: Graph Networks, Robust Machine Learning, and Privacy Computing.

GTML-Lab was set up to investigate fundamental techniques in graph deep learning, and ensures that the learing systems are robust, fair, interpretable, and can maintain the privacy and confidentiality of sensitive data. Ever since the lab was established, our group have published more than 60 papers over journals such as IEEE TNNLS, TIP, TPDS, ACM TOIS, TKDD, etc. and international conference such as KDD, AAAI, IJCAI, ICML, WWW, INFOCOM, CIKM, ICDM, etc.

In the applications of technique, we has carried out longterm research projects with some leading enterprises including Ping An Bank, WeBank, China Merchants Finance and WeChat.
